V-Cloak

May 21, 2022

V-Cloak: Speech-Preserving, Real-time, User-Agnostic Voice Anonymization

Abstract

Audios contain unique user voiceprint that may be abused by malicious attackers when published on social media or sent via communication channels. In this work, we develop a speech-preserving (timbre, naturalness and semanteme), real-time and unlinkable voice anonymization system, named V-Cloak.

We have conducted extensive experiments on four voice datasets containing English, Chinese, French and Italian languages, with five ASVs and five ASRs of different architectures, demonstrating the effectiveness and efficiency of V-Cloak.

Hopefully, V-Cloak may provide a cloak for us in a prism world.
